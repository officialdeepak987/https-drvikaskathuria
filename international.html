Frequently Asked Questions

What are the early warning signs of a brain tumor? +

Early signs may include frequent headaches, blurred vision, balance issues, memory problems, and seizures. It is important to consult a specialist if symptoms persist.

What causes chronic headaches and migraines? +

Chronic headaches can be caused by stress, poor sleep, hormonal changes, diet, or neurological conditions. A proper diagnosis helps in effective treatment.

Do all spine problems require surgery? +

No, many spine problems can be treated with medication, physiotherapy, and lifestyle changes. Surgery is recommended only when necessary.

What is a slip disc and how is it treated? +

A slip disc occurs when a spinal disc moves out of place, causing pain and nerve pressure. Treatment may include medication, therapy, or surgery depending on severity.

How can I prevent spine-related problems? +

Maintaining good posture, regular exercise, proper lifting techniques, and a healthy lifestyle can help prevent spine issues.

Is online consultation effective for diagnosis? +

Online consultation helps in initial evaluation and guidance. For detailed diagnosis, physical examination and tests may be required.

What tests are required for neurological problems? +

Common tests include MRI, CT scan, X-ray, and neurological examination depending on the condition.

Can nerve pain be treated without surgery? +

Yes, nerve pain can often be managed with medications, physiotherapy, and lifestyle changes without surgery.

How soon can I travel after treatment? +

Travel depends on the type of treatment. Your doctor will guide you on safe travel timing after recovery.

Do you provide follow-up care after treatment? +

Yes, we provide continuous follow-up support and guidance even after your treatment is completed.
