Frequently Asked Questions

What are the symptoms of brain tumor? +

Common symptoms include severe headaches, vomiting, vision problems, seizures, and difficulty in speaking or movement.

When should I consult a neurosurgeon? +

If you experience persistent headaches, back pain, numbness, or neurological issues, consult a specialist immediately.

Is spine surgery safe? +

Yes, with modern technology and experienced surgeons, spine surgery is safe and ensures faster recovery.

How long does recovery take after surgery? +

Recovery time depends on the condition, but minimally invasive procedures allow quicker healing.
